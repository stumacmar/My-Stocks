About refreshing

iOS and Safari suspend background JavaScript to save battery. A pure web app cannot reliably run for 30 minutes in the background — this is an OS restriction, not a bug. The combination of per-stock caching (work survives interruption), incremental trickle refresh (25 oldest on every open), and screen wake lock (during a full run) covers most real-world use. Please keep this tab open during a full refresh.

Reading a candlestick

Each candle is one day (or week) of trading. The body shows where the price opened and closed. The thin wicks above and below show the highest and lowest prices reached.

  • Green = closed higher than it opened
  • Red = closed lower than it opened
  • Long wicks = wide range, volatile day
  • Small body + small wicks = quiet, indecisive day
Methodology

CROCI Formula

Cash Return on Capital Invested = (Operating Income × 0.79 + D&A) / (Total Assets + D&A×5 − Current Liabilities) × 100

Scoring (0–7 points)

1. CROCI > 10%
2. PEG < 1.5 (and > 0)
3. Sortino > 0.5
4. Beta < 1.5
5. Alpha > 0
6. 6M% > 0
7. Analyst consensus = Buy or Strong Buy

RAG Rating

Strong ≥ 6 pts · Watch 4–5 pts · Avoid < 4 pts

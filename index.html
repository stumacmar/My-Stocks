Methodology

CROCI Formula

Cash Return on Capital Invested = (Operating Income × 0.79 + D&A) / (Total Assets + D&A×5 − Current Liabilities) × 100

Scoring (0–7 points)

1. CROCI > 10%
2. PEG < 1.5 (and > 0)
3. Sortino > 0.5
4. Beta < 1.5
5. Alpha > 0
6. 6M% > 0
7. Analyst consensus = Buy or Strong Buy
